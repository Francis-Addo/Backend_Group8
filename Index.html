Group 8 College of Education Admission Portal


Group 8 College of Education School Admission System is a web-based application developed to simplify and automate the student admission process.
The system is designed to replace the traditional manual admission procedures with a more efficient, secure, and user-friendly digital platform.
The system allows prospective students to apply online, submit required information.
It also provides administrative users with tools to manage applications, review student details, and make admission decisions efficiently.
